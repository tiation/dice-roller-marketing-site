About the Project

The D&D Dice Roller Simulator was created by passionate tabletop RPG enthusiasts who wanted to bring the tactile experience of dice rolling into the digital age. Built with SwiftUI for native iOS performance, this app combines the convenience of digital tools with the excitement of traditional dice rolling.

Whether you're a seasoned Dungeon Master managing complex encounters or a new player learning the ropes, our app provides all the dice rolling functionality you need in an intuitive, beautifully designed package.
